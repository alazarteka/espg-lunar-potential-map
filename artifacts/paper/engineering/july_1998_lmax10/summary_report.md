# ESPG Analysis Report

## Overview
Analysis based on temporal spherical harmonic model (lmax=10).
Assumed current density: 1.0 µA/m².

## Global Key Findings
- **High Risk Areas**: 99.3% of the lunar surface experiences potentials exceeding 1 kV for >10% of the time.
- **Resource Potential**: 36.4% of the surface offers expected power densities > 1 mW/m².
